True Path Leads careers

A fast-growing lead generation network focused on Personal Loans
At True Path Leads Inc., we’re redefining the lead generation industry with a smarter, more efficient, and highly scalable approach to personal loan lead generation.

Our model is built on three key pillars:

- Exclusive Lender-First Approach – We prioritize high-intent leads for lenders before sharing with networks, ensuring higher conversion rates and better ROI.

- Social Media-Driven Lead Generation – We leverage strategic partnerships with top media buyers, content creators, and influencers to drive high-quality traffic from platforms like Facebook, Instagram, and YouTube.

- AI-Powered Lead Distribution – Our proprietary tech stack integrates with LMS platforms, credit bureaus, and lenders to optimize lead flow, automate decision-making, and maximize monetization.

Our Vision
We’re on a mission to build the most trustworthy and high-performing lead generation network in the industry. By optimizing AI & Machine Learning for ping tree automation, data monetization, and media spend, we’re creating a future-proof ecosystem that benefits both lenders and consumers.

True Path Leads Inc. is set to be the go-to source for high-quality personal loan leads.

Join us on this journey as we reshape the lead gen space.
